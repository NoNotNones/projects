## Create VPC

1. Access the **AWS Management Console** interface:
   - Locate and click on **VPC**
   - Choose **VPC**

![Create VPC](/images/1/0001.png?featherlight=false&width=90pc)

2. Within the **VPC** interface:
   - Select **Your VPC**
   - Click on **Create VPC**
